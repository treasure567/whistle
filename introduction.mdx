The World Cup is 104 matches across 39 days. 48 nations. Hundreds of
storylines breaking at once — a screamer in one stadium while a penalty is
given in another, a market mispriced for ninety seconds, a goal that will
be talked about for a decade.

You have a job. You have a life. You cannot watch all of it, price all of
it, or be at your screen the minute it matters. By the time you open the
app, the moment has passed.

**whistle gives you three people who are always watching.**

## What whistle is

whistle is a World Cup companion where you don't click buttons — you
**fund agents you trust**, and they work the tournament on your behalf,
onchain on X Layer.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Emma — the Scout" icon="camera" href="/agents/emma">
    Watches for the moments that matter and mints them as keepsakes (ERC-721) so a goal you'll never forget becomes something you actually own.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Jack — the Bookie" icon="chart-line" href="/agents/jack">
    Reads every tie, prices the markets, builds you a budgeted slip, and talks you through the run of play while it happens.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tom — the Manager" icon="clipboard-user" href="/agents/tom">
    Hands you a nation, scouts your opponent, suggests changes, and takes you through a knockout to the final — extra time and penalties included.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

You fund whichever agent fits how you want to experience the tournament.
Funding is a real onchain transaction: `PositionManager.allocate` on
X Layer, in **OKB**. The agent now has a budget and a mandate, and every
meaningful thing it does is a transaction you can verify.

## What's underneath

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Real AI, not if-statements" icon="robot" href="/architecture/ai-layer">
    Matches, reads and briefings are generated by an LLM (OpenAI `gpt-4o-mini`) through a tool-use contract, validated and repaired, with a deterministic engine as a safety net so nothing ever stalls.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real onchain, on X Layer" icon="link" href="/onchain/x-layer">
    Five contracts live on X Layer testnet (chainId 1952). You fund agents, agents act, and the receipts are public. OKB in, positions out.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
