Three short paths. Each one ends with something real on X Layer or a full
match on your screen.

<Note>
You'll need an EVM wallet (OKX Wallet recommended). whistle runs on
**X Layer testnet, chainId 1952**. The app adds and switches the network
for you on connect.
</Note>

## Path 1 — Fund an agent onchain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Open the app and connect your wallet. Accept the network switch to
    X Layer testnet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Fund">
    Go to **Fund**. Pick an agent — Emma, Jack or Tom — and an amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allocate">
    Confirm the prompts (mint test OKB → approve → allocate). The funding
    transaction confirms live; the agent's onchain budget is now set. This
    is a real `PositionManager.allocate` call you can open on the
    [explorer](https://www.okx.com/web3/explorer/xlayer-test).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Path 2 — Simulate a match

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Simulate">
    Pick a home and away nation with the searchable country picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kick off">
    The match is generated by the LLM — scorers, cards, penalties, stats,
    MOTM — and plays back live with commentary, an animated field, and
    crowd audio. Look for the **AI-modelled** badge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bet + read the report">
    Place an OKB bet pre-kickoff, let Jack call it live, and read the full
    match report at full-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Path 3 — Manage a nation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Manager">
    Pick your country and difficulty, then set your formation and XI on the
    landscape pitch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take Tom's briefing">
    Tom scouts your next opponent and suggests changes. Adjust your XI,
    then play.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Win the cup">
    Run the knockout (Round of 16 → Final) against escalating AI. Make subs
    mid-match, settle level ties on penalties, and get Tom's analysis after
    each game.
  </Step>
</Steps>
